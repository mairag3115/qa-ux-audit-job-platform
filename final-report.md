\# Final Report



\## Summary

This project analyzed a job platform from both QA and UX perspectives. The review focused on the main user flow, including job search, filtering, reviewing job details, and applying to positions.



\## Main Findings

\- Some interactions may create confusion during filtering

\- Search behavior should be more explicit

\- Key information in job listings could be presented more clearly

\- Users would benefit from stronger feedback during the application flow

\- The empty results page does not provide a direct action to clear filters or recover the search quickly

\- Returning from the application flow may leave the search results page visually dimmed, affecting navigation clarity

\## QA Contribution

The testing process helped identify potential defects and areas where the platform behavior may not match user expectations.



\## UX Contribution

The UX review highlighted friction points that may affect confidence, clarity, and ease of use.



\## Conclusion

Combining QA and UX analysis provides a broader understanding of product quality. Functional correctness is important, but usability and clarity are also essential for a positive user experience.
